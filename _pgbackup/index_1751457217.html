Mission

DBRA SOSE SEC-5 DWARKA strives to nurture young minds equipped with 21st-century skills and a future-forward mindset to solve environmental, social, cultural, emotional, and economic issues nationally and internationally.

Vision

DBRA SOSE SEC-5 DWARKA aims to develop open-minded, empathetic, dynamic, and confident learners possessing skills like critical thinking, decision-making, multilingualism, interpersonal communication, and leadership.
